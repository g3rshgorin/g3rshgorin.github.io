At the core of ecosystems are patterns
in the behaviors and fates of individuals

These patterns underly everything... ...Collective behavior⏤as seen in flocks, herds and crowds⏤systematically alters the flow of materials, energy and information in ecosystems, from biofilms to cities. Our research seeks to understand how collectives mediate and respond to ecosystem change, by synthesizing high-throughput observational data, laboratory approaches, and mathematical models. We work in a range of contexts, focussed on improving the health of human and animal populations, and enhancing the stability of social-ecological systems under global change.
